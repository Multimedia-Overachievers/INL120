Mission

We are Moonrealm Productions, an indie game studio on quest to create intriguing and entertaining horror games which immerse the player in captivating aesthetics and eerie atmospheres. Currently, we have 3 critically acclaimed titles Welcome Home, Off and Limbo, and we're currently wrapping up development for a fourth game called Creaks. Creaks is due to release on the 24th of December, just in time for Christmas!

As a studio, We strive to bring quality games to our supporters. Our amazing team of highly talented developers and creators alike put the utmost care and detail into our games. We make games that we love and love to play and we hope you will too, because we don't plan on stopping anytime soon.

How to download

  1. Click the game's download button above
  2. Click the "Download Now" button on the itch.io website
  3. Enter your payment details
  4. Click the red "Download" button on the downloads page
  5. After the download has completed extract the .zip file
  6. Run the .exe from the extracted file and you're ready to go!
